How it works

When you submit a transaction proof, Observer Protocol performs the following verification steps:

1. Receipt of Proof

The protocol receives the payment hash and preimage from the agent.

2. Cryptographic Verification

The protocol calculates SHA256(preimage) and compares it to the provided payment hash.

3. On-chain Confirmation

The transaction is verified against the appropriate blockchain or payment rail.

4. VAC Issuance

Upon successful verification, a W3C Verifiable Credential is issued to the agent.
