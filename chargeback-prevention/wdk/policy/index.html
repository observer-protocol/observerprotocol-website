Notify Martina above this amount. Below threshold, Vicky transacts without a tap.

How this is enforced
  • 1. Martina signs the policy locally — Ed25519 signature, never leaves her device.
  • 2. Policy ships to Vicky as a W3C DelegationCredential v2 over agent infrastructure.
  • 3. Vicky carries the credential. Each transaction Vicky attempts presents the credential to the merchant.
  • 4. Merchant verifies signature, scope, rail, amount — locally, no call to OP needed.
  • 5. Above the escalation threshold, the merchant returns a soft-reject with a magic-link package. Martina taps once.
