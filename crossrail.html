x402 · USDC on Base

DENIAL AT THE KEY

No EIP-3009 signature ever exists. The deny is thrown inside the unmodified x402 client; nothing settles, the resource stays 402.

L402 · Lightning

DENIAL AT THE PRE-PAYMENT HOOK

The invoice is refused before payment, from the same shared budget as every other rail.

WDK · USDT on TRON

DENIAL INSIDE THE POLICY ENGINE

The proof

One mandate, three rails, denied on every rail.

Committed transcript, 2026-07-03. One $5.00 rolling-24h mandate, signed once. Consumed across three rails, to exactly $5.00. Then the next attempt on each rail was refused from the same shared budget.

$ one mandate · $5.00 / rolling 24h · one shared cross-rail budget
ALLOW x402 $0.01 USDC via Cloudflare's unmodified x402-proxy-template
ALLOW Lightning 5,940-sat L402 invoice · same ledger
ALLOW TRON 2 USDT TRC-20 through the real WDK policy engine · same ledger
budget consumed: 5.00 of 5.00 · next attempt on each rail:
DENY x402 at the key · ObserverDenyError inside the client · no signature exists · resource stays 402
DENY Lightning at the pre-payment hook
DENY TRON inside the policy engine · the transfer is never invoked

Verified against the production x402.org facilitator; settled on-chain on Base Sepolia (blocks 43667763-64); the buyer wallet holds zero ETH. Conversion rates are attested inside the signed credential (USDC=1, USDT=1, sat=0.0005): no oracle anywhere in the flow.

To our knowledge, the first open enforcement layer spanning both 402 lineages (x402, L402) plus wallet-SDK signers under one credential.

Where it's going

Engines exist today for MPP/Tempo (mppx-op-account, public with its integration) and OWS (ows-op-policy), and the enforcement pattern is demonstrated against Aqua's live policy hook (Bitcoin/Liquid); each joins Crossrail as its integration enters the public set. The Monetization Gateway's native cloudflare:402 scheme is a roadmap engine; we are waitlisted.
