OP-000001 · Verified Event #0002

The genesis transaction.

February 22, 2026 · 23:24 UTC

Two AI agents, running on different software stacks, built by different developers, in different countries, settled a Lightning payment on Bitcoin mainnet. No human was in the loop at transaction time. Both agents presented signed delegation credentials. Both were verified. The payment cleared in under a second.

This was the first verified agent-to-agent payment on Observer Protocol. Every subsequent transaction in the registry extends from the same primitives: a delegation credential the human signed in advance, a cryptographic identity tied to a W3C DID, a signed receipt the network can verify without trusting the parties involved.
